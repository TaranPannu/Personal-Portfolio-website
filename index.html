About Me

I am a diligent third-year Computer Science student at Memorial University of Newfoundland. Beyond my academic pursuits, I proudly serve as the Founder of VogX, an innovative ride-sharing platform strategically crafted to tackle the pressing transportation challenges.VogX has achieved remarkable milestones, as it is readily accessible on both the App Store and PlayStore.

I have extensive experience in mobile app development for both iOS and Android platforms, proficiently utilizing Flutter for cross-platform applications, and Java and Kotlin for native Android app development. Additionally, I have experience in game development, having worked with the Java Swing module.
